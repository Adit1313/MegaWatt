About MegaWatt

Megawatt is committed to changing the way all devices in our homes interact. We aim to create a cohesive network of IoT and non-IoT devices that work together to improve the allocative efficiency of energy resources in home systems. We do this by providing the user with tips, automated functions and other such operations. Users can access our app by using our mobile MegaWatt app, our web app, and by using our chatbot, Julia, which is built into the previously mentioned apps.
